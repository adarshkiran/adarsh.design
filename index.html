About Me

I'm an artist from India with over 13 years of creative industry experience. I hold a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Applied Arts. My goal is to turn business ideas into strong concepts and build user-friendly, high-quality digital experiences. I focus on creating innovative, intuitive, and visually appealing designs by using new styles and methods.
Apart from work I like to trek and explore the mountains.

What I Do

visual visual I explore the internet for visual references and blend them together to create my own unique work.
motion motion I use motion to make my designs feel more dynamic than they actually are, bringing them to life.
product product I stick to solid design principles, test things out, tweak a bunch… and cross my fingers it works.
illustration illustration Illustrate with intention, combining imagination with just enough detail to tell a story that feels alive.
photography photography I'm passionate about photography, trekking mountains, and meeting new people, capturing moments along the journey.
